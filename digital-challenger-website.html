Your 7-Week Journey

Each week builds on the last - no going back!

1
Foundation

Value Creation & Business Model

Transform how your business creates and captures value. Move beyond selling products to building platforms.

Possible Topics (Slides 1-6)

  • Vision and transformation thesis
  • What's broken with the traditional model
  • New value propositions
  • Business model innovation
  • Revenue stream ideas
  • Basic economics and viability
2
Understanding

Consumer in Times of Crisis

Decode how economic uncertainty reshapes behavior. Build recession-proof value propositions.

Possible Topics (Slides 7-11)

  • How crisis impacts your industry
  • Customer personas and their adaptations
  • Changed behaviors and needs
  • Price sensitivity considerations
  • New value perceptions
3-4
Intelligence & Tech

AI Integration & Technical Architecture

Design AI features that create real value. Build the technical foundation that makes it all work.

Possible Topics (Slides 12-21)

  • AI features and use cases for your business
  • Addressing algorithm aversion and building trust
  • Personalization strategies
  • Technical infrastructure overview
  • API connections and integrations
  • GenAI applications and prompts
  • Data strategy and privacy
  • Cost considerations
5
Channels

Digital Channels & Mobile

Design omnichannel experiences. Bridge physical and digital. Make mobile your superpower.

Possible Topics (Slides 22-26)

  • Channel strategy and priorities
  • Mobile app concepts and features
  • Website and platform considerations
  • Physical-digital integration ideas
  • Social commerce opportunities
6
Launch

SEO, SEA & Go-to-Market

Dominate search. Launch with impact. Build momentum from day one.

Possible Topics (Slides 27-31)

  • Launch strategy and campaign ideas
  • Search strategy (organic and paid)
  • Target keywords and positioning
  • Marketing channels and budget ideas
  • Content and social media approach
  • Success metrics to track
7
Experience

Customer Journey & Retention

Map the path from stranger to evangelist. Build community. Create lasting relationships.

Possible Topics (Slides 32-40)

  • Customer journey mapping
  • Key touchpoints and interactions
  • Retention and loyalty strategies
  • Community building ideas
  • Implementation timeline
  • Risk considerations
  • Executive summary
  • Next steps and priorities

Domino's Pizza

From Worst Pizza to Tech Powerhouse

1

Value Creation

Redefined as "tech company that delivers pizza" - built proprietary ordering platform worth billions

2

Crisis Consumer

Won 2008 recession with $5.99 deals + zero-friction ordering when dining out became luxury

3-4

AI & Technical Implementation

"Dom" the pizza bot, AI quality control cameras, predictive ordering, voice assistants, open platform APIs

5

Digital Channels

First to master mobile (70% of orders), tested drones, autonomous vehicles

6

Marketing

"Oh Yes We Did" campaign turned tech features into stories, dominated "pizza near me"

7

Journey

Made pizza tracker entertainment, one-click reorder, gamification, Pizza Profile rewards

Result: Stock price from $7 (2010) to $567 (2021) | Became world's largest pizza company

Nike

From Shoe Maker to Digital Fitness Ecosystem

1

Value Creation

Shifted from "we sell shoes" to "we make you a better athlete" - performance improvement platform

2

Crisis Consumer

When COVID closed gyms, Nike Training Club became the home gym, kept revenue flowing

3-4

AI & Technical Infrastructure

Nike Fit scans feet with phone camera, AI coaching in Run Club, Nike+ platform connects with Apple Watch, Strava, Spotify

5

Digital Channels

SNKRS app created shoe drops as events, Nike app for members, stores as experiences

6

Marketing

Owns athlete-related search, influencer army, turned products into content

7

Journey

Inspire ‚Üí Train ‚Üí Gear Up ‚Üí Achieve ‚Üí Share ‚Üí Repeat - lifetime value focus

Result: Direct-to-consumer from 15% to 40% of revenue | $50B+ valuation

Value Creation

Warby Parker

From Online Experiment to Eyewear Revolution

1

Value Creation

Cut out middlemen, added social mission (buy one, give one), designer quality at $95

2

Crisis Consumer

Thrived in recession - $95 glasses vs $400 at traditional stores, payment plans

3-4

AI & Technical Platform

Virtual try-on, face shape analysis, prescription check app, integrated with vision insurance platforms

5

Digital Channels

Started online-only, stores became showrooms, app for virtual try-on, telehealth

6

Marketing

Dominated "buy glasses online" before category existed, referral program, content

7

Journey

Quiz ‚Üí Home Try-On (5 pairs free) ‚Üí Purchase ‚Üí Share ‚Üí Annual Reminder ‚Üí Lifetime

Result: $6B valuation | Forced entire industry to change | Proved digital-first retail works
